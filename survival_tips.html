Survival Tips

  • Find Shelter: Seek shelter to protect yourself from the elements. Use natural formations like caves or build a makeshift shelter using branches and leaves.
  • Stay Hydrated: Ensure you have access to clean drinking water. Boil water if necessary, or use water purification tablets.
  • Keep Warm: In cold conditions, keep warm with layers of clothing and by building a fire. Insulate yourself from the ground with leaves or a sleeping pad.
  • Signal for Help: Use a whistle, mirror, or other signaling devices to attract attention.
  • First Aid: Carry a first aid kit and know basic first aid techniques.
  • Food Supply: Pack non-perishable food items and know how to find edible plants and animals.
